## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:
- Understand the regulatory landscape for financial technology services

## Introduction

Financial technology (FinTech) regulation and compliance represents one of the most dynamic and challenging areas in modern finance. As technology continues to transform financial services, regulators worldwide are working to balance innovation promotion with consumer protection, financial stability, and market integrity. Understanding and navigating this regulatory landscape is crucial for FinTech entrepreneurs, established financial institutions, and compliance professionals.
