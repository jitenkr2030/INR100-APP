# Module 23: Advanced Tax Planning and Strategies

## Overview
Master advanced tax planning techniques including tax-loss harvesting, capital gains optimization, estate planning tax strategies, and international tax implications for high-net-worth individuals.

## Learning Objectives
- Master tax-loss harvesting strategies
- Optimize capital gains and losses
- Understand international tax implications
- Learn estate planning tax strategies
- Explore advanced deduction techniques
- Create tax-efficient investment portfolios

## Course Structure

### Lesson 23.1: Tax-Loss Harvesting Fundamentals
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Harvesting mechanics, wash sale rules, optimization strategies
- **Interactive**: Tax-loss harvesting calculator
- **Case Study**: Year-end tax optimization

### Lesson 23.2: Capital Gains Optimization
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Short-term vs long-term gains, timing strategies, holding periods
- **Interactive**: Capital gains calculator
- **Assessment**: Optimal strategy analyzer

### Lesson 23.3: International Tax Planning
- **Duration selling**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Foreign income taxation, DTAA benefits, compliance requirements
- **Interactive**: International tax calculator
- **Resources**: Country-wise tax treaty guide

### Lesson 23.4: Estate Planning and Wealth Transfer
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Estate taxes, gift taxation, trust structures, will planning
- **Interactive**: Estate planning calculator
- **Tools**: Wealth transfer optimizer

### Lesson 23.5: Business Tax Optimization
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Corporate tax planning, business expense optimization, depreciation
- **Interactive**: Business tax calculator
- **Case Study**: Startup tax planning

### Lesson 23.6: Advanced Investment Tax Strategies
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Tax-efficient fund selection, asset location, retirement accounts
- **Interactive**: Tax-efficient portfolio builder
- **Assessment**: Complete tax strategy evaluation
