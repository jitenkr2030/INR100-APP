# Module 19: International Investing and Global Markets

## Overview
Expand your investment horizons beyond Indian markets. Learn to invest in global markets through ADRs, international mutual funds, ETFs, and direct foreign investment while managing currency risks and understanding international regulations.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand global market dynamics and opportunities
- Learn ADR and international fund investment strategies
- Master currency risk management techniques
- Explore emerging market investment opportunities
- Understand tax implications of international investing
- Build diversified global portfolios

## Course Structure

### Lesson 19.1: Introduction to Global Markets
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Major global markets, economic indicators, market correlations
- **Interactive**: Global market comparison tool
- **Resources**: Market timing dashboard

### Lesson 19.2: American Depositary Receipts (ADRs)
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: ADR structure, trading mechanisms, popular ADRs
- **Interactive**: ADR selection and analysis tool
- **Case Study**: Investing in US tech giants via ADRs

### Lesson 19.3: International Mutual Funds and ETFs
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Fund types, expense ratios, performance analysis
- **Interactive**: International fund comparison calculator
- **Assessment**: Fund selection framework

### Lesson 19.4: Currency Risk Management
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Hedging strategies, currency ETFs, timing considerations
- **Interactive**: Currency exposure calculator
- **Tools**: Hedging cost calculator

### Lesson 19.5: Emerging Markets Investment
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: BRICS, ASEAN, Latin American opportunities
- **Interactive**: Emerging market analyzer
- **Case Study**: Vietnam and Indonesia growth stories

### Lesson 19.6: International Portfolio Construction
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Asset allocation, rebalancing, regulatory compliance
- **Interactive**: Global portfolio optimizer
- **Resources**: International investment checklist

## Prerequisites

- Module 12 (Stock Market Analysis) recommended
