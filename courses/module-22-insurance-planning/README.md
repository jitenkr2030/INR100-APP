# Module 22: Comprehensive Insurance Planning

## Overview
Master the art of insurance planning for complete financial security. Learn about term life insurance, health insurance, disability insurance, property insurance, and how to integrate insurance into your overall financial plan.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand insurance fundamentals and risk management
- Learn to calculate appropriate life insurance coverage
- Master health insurance selection and claims
- Explore disability and critical illness insurance
- Understand property and liability insurance
- Create comprehensive insurance portfolios

## Course Structure

### Lesson 22.1: Insurance Fundamentals
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Risk management, insurance principles, coverage types
- **Interactive**: Risk assessment calculator
- **Resources**: Insurance needs analyzer

### Lesson 22.2: Term Life Insurance Mastery
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Term vs whole life, coverage calculation, policy selection
- **Interactive**: Life insurance calculator
- **Case Study**: Family protection planning

### Lesson 22.3: Health Insurance Planning
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Individual vs family plans, coverage optimization, claims
- **Interactive**: Health insurance premium calculator
- **Assessment**: Health insurance comparison matrix

### Lesson 22.4: Critical Illness and Disability Insurance
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Critical illness coverage, disability insurance, income protection
- **Interactive**: Critical illness planning tool
- **Resources**: Disability insurance guide

### Lesson 22.5: Property and Liability Insurance
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Home insurance, vehicle insurance, liability coverage
- **Interactive**: Property insurance calculator
- **Case Study**: Complete insurance portfolio

### Lesson 22.6: Insurance as Investment Tool
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: ULIPs, endowment plans, insurance-linked investments
- **Interactive**: Insurance investment analyzer
- **Assessment**: Insurance vs investment comparison
