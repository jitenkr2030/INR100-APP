# Module 20: Sustainable Investing and ESG

## Overview
Learn to align your investments with your values while generating positive returns. Master ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) investing, green bonds, impact investing, and sustainable finance strategies for the future.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand ESG investment principles and frameworks
- Learn to evaluate companies on ESG criteria
- Explore green bonds and sustainable investment options
- Master impact investing strategies
- Understand climate risk and sustainable finance
- Build ESG-aligned portfolios

## Course Structure

### Lesson 20.1: ESG Fundamentals and Frameworks
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: ESG criteria, rating agencies, investment approaches
- **Interactive**: ESG scoring calculator
- **Resources**: ESG framework comparison

### Lesson 20.2: Environmental Investing
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Clean energy, climate tech, carbon credits
- **Interactive**: Environmental impact calculator
- **Case Study**: Renewable energy investment analysis

### Lesson 20.3: Social Impact Investing
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Healthcare, education, social enterprises
- **Interactive**: Social impact measurement tool
- **Assessment**: Impact vs return analysis

### Lesson 20.4: Governance and Corporate Responsibility
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Board diversity, executive compensation, transparency
- **Interactive**: Governance scoring system
- **Case Study**: Corporate governance transformations

### Lesson 20.5: Green Bonds and Climate Finance
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Green bond mechanics, climate funds, carbon markets
- **Interactive**: Green bond calculator
- **Resources**: Green bond issuer database

### Lesson 20.6: Building Sustainable Portfolios
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Portfolio construction, performance measurement, reporting
- **Interactive**: Sustainable portfolio optimizer
- **Tools**: ESG reporting dashboard
