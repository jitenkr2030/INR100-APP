## Robo-Advisor Fundamentals

### What are Robo-Advisors?
Robo-advisors are digital platforms that provide automated, algorithm-driven financial planning services with minimal human intervention. They use sophisticated algorithms to manage investments, rebalance portfolios, and optimize tax strategies based on individual investor goals and risk tolerance.

### Core Technology Components

#### Algorithmic Portfolio Management
- **Modern Portfolio Theory**: Algorithm-based asset allocation
- **Risk Assessment**: Automated risk profiling
- **Rebalancing**: Systematic portfolio rebalancing
- **Tax Optimization**: Tax-loss harvesting automation
- **Goal Tracking**: Goal-based investment tracking

#### User Interface and Experience
- **Digital Platforms**: Web and mobile interfaces
- **Questionnaires**: Automated risk assessment questionnaires
- **Dashboards**: Investment tracking dashboards
- **Notifications**: Automated alerts and updates
- **Education**: Built-in financial education

#### Data and Analytics
- **Market Data**: Real-time market data integration
- **Personal Data**: Investor profile and preference data
- **Behavioral**: Behavioral finance algorithms
- **Predictive**: Predictive analytics for planning
- **Optimization**: Continuous portfolio optimization

### Robo-Advisor vs Traditional Advisory

| Aspect | Robo-Advisors | Traditional Advisors |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Cost** | Low fees (0.25-0.75%) | High fees (1-2%+) |
| **Minimum** | Low or no minimums | High minimums ($100K+) |
| **Accessibility** | 24/7 online access | Business hours, appointments |
| **Personalization** | Algorithm-based | Human relationship-based |
| **Complexity** | Simple to moderate | Complex financial planning |
| **Technology** | Advanced technology | Limited technology |
| **Scalability** | Highly scalable | Limited scalability |
| **Human Touch** | Minimal human interaction | High human interaction |

- **Minimum**: No minimum investment

- **Minimum**: $500 minimum investment

#### Schwab Intelligent Portfolios

- **Features**: No advisory fees, ETF portfolios

- **Minimum**: $5,000 minimum investment
