By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

## Introduction

Insurance risk management is the systematic process of identifying, assessing, monitoring, and controlling risks that affect an insurance organization's ability to meet its strategic objectives. Given the fundamental nature of risk in insurance business, robust risk management is not just regulatory compliance—it's the cornerstone of sustainable insurance operations, financial stability, and stakeholder value creation.
