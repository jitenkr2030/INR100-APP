## Introduction

Insurance Technology (InsurTech) represents the application of advanced technologies to enhance, disrupt, or enable new insurance business models and processes. From AI-powered underwriting and blockchain-based claims processing to IoT-driven risk assessment and mobile-first customer experiences, InsurTech is fundamentally transforming how insurance products are designed, distributed, underwritten, and serviced in the digital age.
