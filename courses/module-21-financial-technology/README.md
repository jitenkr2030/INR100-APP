# Module 21: Financial Technology and Digital Finance

## Overview
Navigate the rapidly evolving world of financial technology. Learn about robo-advisors, digital payment systems, blockchain applications, algorithmic trading, and the future of finance technology.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand fintech ecosystem and innovations
- Learn to use robo-advisors and automated investing
- Master digital payment systems and cryptocurrency
- Explore algorithmic trading and quantitative finance
- Understand regulatory technology (RegTech)
- Prepare for the future of finance

## Course Structure

### Lesson 21.1: Fintech Ecosystem Overview
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Fintech categories, major players, innovation trends
- **Interactive**: Fintech landscape mapper
- **Resources**: Fintech company database

### Lesson 21.2: Robo-Advisors and Automated Investing
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Robo-advisor mechanics, algorithm-based portfolio management
- **Interactive**: Robo-advisor comparison tool
- **Case Study**: Automated vs human portfolio management

### Lesson 21.3: Digital Payment Systems
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: UPI, digital wallets, CBDCs, payment gateways
- **Interactive**: Payment system analyzer
- **Assessment**: Digital payment security audit

### Lesson 21.4: Blockchain Applications in Finance
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: Smart contracts, DeFi protocols, tokenization
- **Interactive**: Blockchain transaction simulator
- **Tools**: DeFi yield calculator

### Lesson 21.5: Algorithmic Trading and Quant Finance
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Algorithmic strategies, backtesting, risk models
- **Interactive**: Trading algorithm builder
- **Resources**: Quantitative strategy library

### Lesson 21.6: Future of Finance Technology
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: AI in finance, open banking, embedded finance
- **Interactive**: Future finance scenarios
- **Assessment**: Technology adoption strategy
