# Module 18: Social Security and Government Schemes

## Overview
Master India's comprehensive social security ecosystem and government-backed investment schemes. Learn to maximize benefits from PPF, EPF, NPS, Sukanya Samriddhi, and other schemes for optimal wealth building and retirement planning.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand India's social security framework
- Maximize benefits from PPF, EPF, and NPS schemes
- Learn Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana for girl child planning
- Explore Atal Pension Yojana and other pension schemes
- Master tax benefits and withdrawal strategies
- Create integrated financial plans using government schemes

## Course Structure

### Lesson 18.1: India's Social Security Framework
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Overview of social security systems, government initiatives
- **Interactive**: Social security benefits calculator
- **Resources**: Scheme comparison matrix

### Lesson 18.2: Public Provident Fund (PPF) Mastery
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: PPF rules, tax benefits, withdrawal strategies
- **Interactive**: PPF calculator with compound growth
- **Case Study**: 15-year PPF investment journey

### Lesson 18.3: Employee Provident Fund (EPF) Optimization
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: EPF contributions, interest rates, withdrawal rules
- **Interactive**: EPF growth calculator
- **Tools**: EPF withdrawal planning tool

### Lesson 18.4: National Pension System (NPS) Deep Dive
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: NPS tiers, fund selection, annuity options
- **Interactive**: NPS retirement corpus calculator
- **Assessment**: NPS vs other pension schemes analysis

### Lesson 18.5: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana for Girl Child
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Content**: SSY rules, benefits, investment strategies
- **Interactive**: SSY maturity calculator
- **Case Study**: Planning for daughter's education and marriage

### Lesson 18.6: Atal Pension Yojana and Other Schemes
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: APY, PMJJBY, PMSBY, and other social security schemes
- **Interactive**: Social security portfolio optimizer
- **Resources**: Complete scheme handbook
