# Module 17: Cryptocurrency and Digital Assets

## Overview
This comprehensive module covers the emerging world of cryptocurrency and digital assets, providing students with essential knowledge to navigate this rapidly evolving financial landscape safely and effectively.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand blockchain technology fundamentals
- Learn about major cryptocurrencies and their use cases
- Master cryptocurrency investment strategies and risk management
- Explore DeFi (Decentralized Finance) opportunities
- Understand regulatory landscape and compliance requirements
- Develop skills for secure cryptocurrency storage and trading

## Course Structure

### Lesson 17.1: Introduction to Blockchain Technology
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Blockchain basics, distributed ledger technology, consensus mechanisms
- **Interactive**: Blockchain visualization tool
- **Calculator**: Mining profitability calculator

### Lesson 17.2: Understanding Bitcoin and Major Cryptocurrencies
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Bitcoin history, altcoins, stablecoins, tokenomics
- **Interactive**: Cryptocurrency comparison tool
- **Case Study**: Bitcoin adoption journey

### Lesson 17.3: Cryptocurrency Investment Strategies
- **Duration**: 35 minutes
- **Content**: DCA strategies, portfolio allocation, risk management
- **Interactive**: Crypto portfolio calculator
- **Assessment**: Investment scenario analysis

### Lesson 17.4: DeFi and Decentralized Finance
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Content**: Yield farming, liquidity mining, lending protocols
- **Interactive**: DeFi yield calculator
- **Case Study**: DeFi protocol analysis

### Lesson 17.5: Security and Storage Solutions
- **Duration**: 25 minutes
- **Content**: Wallets, cold storage, security best practices
- **Interactive**: Security checklist tool
- **Resources**: Security guides and templates

### Lesson 17.6: Regulatory Compliance and Taxation
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Content**: Global regulations, tax implications, compliance requirements
- **Interactive**: Tax calculation tools
- **Resources**: Regulatory update tracker

## Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of traditional investing
- Module 1-4 completion recommended
- Basic computer literacy
